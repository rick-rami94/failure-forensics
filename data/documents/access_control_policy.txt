1. Purpose
This policy establishes requirements for granting, reviewing, and revoking logical
access to Northwind Logistics information systems, in support of control family
Access Control (AC).

2. Scope
Applies to all employees, contractors, and service accounts accessing internal systems.

3. Requirements
3.1 Account Management (AC-2): Access is provisioned on a least-privilege basis and
    approved by the system owner. Accounts are disabled within 24 hours of separation.
3.2 Access Enforcement (AC-3): Systems enforce role-based access control.
3.3 Least Privilege (AC-6): Administrative privileges are granted only where required
    and reviewed quarterly.

4. Review
Account entitlements are recertified quarterly. This policy is reviewed annually.
Residual risk after controls: Low.
