Summary
During testing of the Account Management control (AC-2), auditors found that 12 of 40
sampled separated employees retained active accounts beyond the 24-hour disablement
window required by the Logical Access Control Policy.

Risk Rating: High
Likelihood: Likely
Impact: Major (unauthorized access to logistics scheduling systems)

Recommendation
Automate deprovisioning via the HR-to-IAM joiner/mover/leaver workflow and add a daily
reconciliation report. Reassess control effectiveness after remediation.
